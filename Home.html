Welcome to CLCI

Join us in our journey to explore the world of chess.

Chess Lovers Club Indore (CLCI) is a community of chess enthusiasts in Indore, India. Our mission is to promote chess through regular meetups, tournaments, and training sessions. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, CLCI is the place for you.

Join us to improve your chess skills, participate in tournaments, and connect with fellow chess lovers.
